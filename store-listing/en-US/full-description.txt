Use your wireless earbuds on Galaxy as smoothly as on iOS.

🔋 Battery at a Glance
See left/right earbud and case battery on the home screen, in the status bar, and as a home-screen widget.

📦 Case Open Notification
Open the case and instantly see battery levels in a notification.

👂 In-Ear Auto Pause
Music pauses when you remove an earbud and resumes when you put it back.

🔄 Smart Auto Connect
Detects nearby earbuds and shows a quick-connect notification.

📍 Last Known Location
Records the last spot where the connection dropped (optional, helps when earbuds are misplaced).

🛡️ Galaxy Power Saver Wizard
Auto-detects your One UI version and walks you through the right battery exception so background tracking doesn't get killed.

🌏 Native Korean Support
All UI, notifications, and help text written natively in Korean.

✅ Free, No Ads, No Tracking
No data leaves your device. Everything is processed locally.

🎧 Supported Earbuds
- AirPods 2nd / 3rd / 4 (incl. ANC)
- AirPods Pro 1st / 2nd (Lightning/USB-C) / 3rd
- AirPods Max
- Galaxy S21 or newer (One UI 3.0+ recommended)

⚙️ Permissions
- Bluetooth — receive earbud signals (no location inference)
- Notifications — case open / battery alerts
- Location (optional) — only when you enable Last Known Location
- Full permission rationale inside the app under "Permissions"
