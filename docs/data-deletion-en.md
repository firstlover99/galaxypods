# Data Deletion Request

GalaxyPods **does not store any user data on external servers.** All data is processed on your device only.

## How to Delete Your Data

### Method 1. In-App Settings

1. Open GalaxyPods
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (top-right) → **Manage stored data**
3. Tap **Delete last location** → done immediately

### Method 2. Uninstall the App

1. Long-press the GalaxyPods icon on your home screen
2. Select **Uninstall**
3. All data on your device (settings, last location, etc.) will be deleted automatically

### Method 3. Android System Settings

1. Android **Settings** → **Apps** → **GalaxyPods**
2. **Storage** → **Clear data** or **Clear storage**

---

## No Server-Side Data

This app strictly follows these policies.

- ✅ **No collection** of identifying information
- ✅ **No transmission** of location, battery, or pairing history
- ✅ **No analytics or ad SDKs** (opt-out provided if Crashlytics is later adopted)
- ✅ **No user accounts** required

Therefore, there is no separate "server-side data deletion request" process. Deleting from your device is sufficient.
